# Sakura Core - Project Brief

## Product
Sakura Core is a lightweight ERP-style operational system for small manufacturing teams, with a focus on sheet metal production workflows.

## Mission
Reduce chaos between departments and production areas by making status, ownership, and next actions visible in one flow.

## Core Value
- Fast deployment
- Lean operations
- Low training overhead
- Minimal manual entry
- Lower risk of human error

## Target Audience
- Small and medium manufacturing companies
- Teams with fragmented communication between engineering, production, and managers
- Companies that want quick operational control without heavy enterprise ERP rollout

## Commercial Model (Initial)
- One-time setup/launch fee for core module
- Monthly subscription for core module
- Optional add-on modules billed monthly per enabled module

## Time Target
- Product maturity target for initial commercial rollout: before New Year
