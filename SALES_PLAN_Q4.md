# Sakura Core - Sales Plan Q4

## Positioning
Operational control system for small sheet metal manufacturing teams.

## Offer Shape (Draft)
- Core setup (one-time)
- Core monthly subscription
- Optional add-on modules monthly

## Suggested Pricing Range (Draft)
- One-time setup: EUR 1,500-4,000
- Core monthly: EUR 120-350
- Add-on module monthly: EUR 40-150 per module

## Funnel Targets
- Weekly outbound via LinkedIn + direct outreach
- Demo-first messaging with concrete pain points:
  - status chaos
  - unclear ownership
  - delayed decisions

## Q4 KPI Targets (Draft)
- 50-75 targeted contacts/week
- 4-6 demos/week
- 1-2 proposals/week
- 1 new client every 2-3 weeks after warm-up

## Scenario Planning
- Conservative: 3 clients by year end
- Base: 6-8 clients
- Strong: 10+ clients with expanding module MRR

## Execution Rule
Sell outcomes, not features:
"less chaos, faster decisions, visible production status."
